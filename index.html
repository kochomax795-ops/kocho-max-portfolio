Deep-dives into three standout projects that demonstrate my technical scope and product mindset.

Emergency Response Platform

Real-time dispatch for medical services

A mobile-first emergency response platform built for the Ugandan market that connects dispatchers, responders, and patients with live location tracking.

  • Context: Delivered in 12 weeks for local emergency services while leading the product architecture and implementation.
  • Problem: Existing dispatch workflows were manual, slow, and disconnected from live responder location data.
  • Process: Mapped user journeys, built wireframes for mobile dispatch flows, and implemented Firebase realtime updates.
  • Solution: Launched a React Native app with GPS tracking, route guidance, Twilio notifications, and secure backend APIs.
  • Results: Pilot tests showed a 30% faster response handoff and reduced coordination delays.

Music Streaming SaaS

Creator platform for streaming and payments

A music SaaS product with playlists, secure playback, and Flutterwave payment integration for artist monetization.

  • Context: Built as a scalable product concept for independent musicians and local creators.
  • Problem: Artists lacked a trusted platform for streaming, payments, and revenue tracking in Uganda.
  • Process: Developed UI mockups, designed relational data models, and integrated payment checkout flows.
  • Solution: Delivered a React frontend, Node.js API, and MySQL backend with secure user authentication.
  • Results: Launched a reusable SaaS prototype suited for early adopters and digital content marketplaces.
